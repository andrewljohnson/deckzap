DeckZap Manifesto

CCG is an anti-pattern for making the largest community for competitive card games.

  • Everyone should have access to the best cards.
  • There should be paid and free-to-play options.
  • Paid options should focus on what formats you can play, not what cards you can access.

Players should be involved in building cards/formats.

  • Players should directly impact the game and cards, not just decks.
  • Players should be able to script/code to change the game() or make new game(s).
  • Most players don't like to build decks.

AI is important to card games.

  • Cards can be better balanced by bots.
  • Superhuman bot quality is possible.
  • Bots can identify the best decks to ease the work fo a new metagame and make new formats more fun.
